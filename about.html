    At Suraksha , is a platform dedicated to women's safety and empowerment, aimed at creating a fear-free society. This platform offers the best educational support for women in both urban and rural areas, addressing the critical issue of women's safety due to increasing violence, particularly in urban settings.

    Ensuring women's safety in smart cities is essential, yet it continues to be a significant global challenge, despite technological advancements. Although law enforcement keeps criminal records, they are not readily accessible to the public.

    Our Vision

    Safer spaces for all, especially women and girls, so that they can explore and achieve their potential.
