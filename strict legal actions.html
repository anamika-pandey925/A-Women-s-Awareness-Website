Women's Safety and Strict Legal Actions

Ensuring Women's Safety

Women's safety is a fundamental aspect of building a just and equal society. Laws and measures have been put in place to protect women against violence and harassment. It is crucial for everyone to be aware of their rights and the available legal provisions.

Strict Legal Actions

  • Strict punishment for crimes like domestic violence, sexual harassment, and stalking under laws such as IPC Section 354 and 498A.
  • Implementation of workplace safety measures under the Sexual Harassment of Women at Workplace (Prevention, Prohibition, and Redressal) Act, 2013.
  • Fast-track courts for speedy trial of cases involving violence against women.
  • Emergency helplines and safety apps for immediate assistance.

Know Your Rights

Women have the right to report harassment or violence without fear. Laws are designed to ensure their privacy and dignity during legal proceedings.
