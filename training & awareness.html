Skill Development Initiatives for Women

1. Skill India Mission

Objective: Empower women through skill training across various sectors.

Features:

  • Offers free or subsidized training.
  • Includes digital literacy, soft skills, and vocational training.
  • Online learning modules via platforms like the National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC).

Website: NSDC

2. Mahila Shakti Kendra (MSK)

Objective: Promote digital literacy, entrepreneurship, and employment for rural women.

Features:

  • Online and offline training programs for skill development.
  • Focuses on vocational skills like tailoring, handicrafts, and computer operations.

Website: Women & Child Development Ministry
